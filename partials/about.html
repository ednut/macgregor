About Us At

MacGregor Heritage Foundation.

The MacGregor Heritage Foundation is a not-for-profit charitable organization dedicated to providing support for children through education and healthcare. At the core of its mission is the principle of empowerment and poverty alleviation.

The Foundation was established in 2018 by Professor (Dr.) Alexander O. MacGregor, a world-renown pharmaceutical technology expert, educator and successful business entrepreneur. Professor MacGregor's charitable heritage can be traced back to over 150 years of philanthropy started by his grand-father, Chief (Captain) G.B. MacGregor(ca.1863-1965), a World-War I veteran in the late nineteenth century and which continued with his father, Chief Harry Adebisi MacGregor (ca.1934-2011), an investigative journalist and merchant industrialist in the late twentieth and early twenty-first century.

MacGregor Heritage Children's Foundation (MHCF)

MacGregor Heritage Children's Foundation is an international relief and development organization dedicated to working and providing education to children from impoverished families and communities to overcome poverty and unjust marginalization.

Our Mission

To empower and create a future of hope for children, by helping them develop the skills and resources to overcome poverty and pursue prosperity for their familiesand communities.

Our Vision

We envision of a world where every child, regardless of their socio-economic status, has a voice and the ability to achieve their full potential.
