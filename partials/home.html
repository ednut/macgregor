Our Mission
To empower and create a future of hope for children, by helping them develop the skills and resources to overcome poverty and pursue prosperity for their familiesand communities.
The Foundation was established in 2018 by Professor (Dr.) Alexander O. MacGregor, a world-renown pharmaceutical technology expert, educator and successful business entrepreneur. Professor MacGregor's charitable heritage can be traced back to over 150 years of philanthropy started by his grand-father, Chief (Captain) G.B. MacGregor(ca.1863-1965), a World-War I veteran
